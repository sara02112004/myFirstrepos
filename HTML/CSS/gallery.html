Pizza hut

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin, consisting of a flattened disk of bread dough topped with various ingredients such as tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, olives, and herbs, and baked quickly in a high-temperature oven. The term "pizza" was first recorded in AD 997, and it has evolved into a global phenomenon with numerous variations, including Neapolitan pizza, which is made with buffalo mozzarella and San Marzano tomatoes. Pizza is popular in many forms, including restaurant-style, take-and-bake, and grocery-store options, and is known for its versatility and appeal across different cultures.
